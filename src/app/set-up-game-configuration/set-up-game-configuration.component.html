Welcome to Music Trivia!

For Music Trivia, you have two choices:

  1. You can choose a genre above and then for each question a song will play. Your challenge is to guess the correct name of the song for the music snippet.
  2. You can choose the album challenge. Enter in the Album search query the name of the album you want to try. There will be no sound for this; rather for the album you pick, for each question there will be 1 song from that album you have to identify amongst other answer choices that will consist of songs not from that album.

Option Settings:

  • You can modify the difficulty by choosing how many possible answer choices you want to play with. You can choose from 2-5.
  • You can choose how many questions you want, anywhere from 2-8.
